Breaking Sports News: IPL 2025 Suspended Amid Escalating India-Pakistan Tensions

In a significant development, the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has announced the suspension of the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025 for one week, effective immediately, due to escalating military tensions between India and Pakistan. The decision follows recent cross-border incidents and is aimed at ensuring the safety of players, staff, and fans.

Breaking News: Indian Stock Markets Tumble Amid Escalating Indo-Pak Tensions

Breaking Tech News: FAA Accelerates Air Traffic Control Upgrades After Newark Radar Outage

On Friday morning, Newark Liberty International Airport experienced a significant technology outage, marking the second such incident in two weeks. At approximately 3:55 a.m., a radar and communications blackout occurred at the Philadelphia air-traffic control facility, lasting about 90 seconds. This disruption led to approximately 25% of flights at Newark being delayed and 10% canceled by midday.
